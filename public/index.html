About Me

My name is Ken Dickey and I come from a career in Opticianry. Learning software development for me was not started as a decision, but rather a curiosity. As I fed this curiosity and made it into a hobby, this led to a point where I needed to make a decision. That decision was whether or not I wanted to continue my slow progression, or step out of my comfort zone. I chose this field not only because of the sense of accomplishment from completing projects, but also because of the vast unknown out there that I'm eager to explore. With new development opportunities expanding every day, the possibilities are endless. Ideally, I would like to join a small team that utilizes multiple platforms for real world applications. In my journey up to now, I have had roles in industries that require fast problem solving skills. Through hard work and innate reasoning prowess, I hope to fast become a better developer, and hope the same for you.
